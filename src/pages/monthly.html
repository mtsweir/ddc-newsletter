Monthly News Roundup
Vol 157, September 2017

Featured News

Lilly's new targeted breast cancer agent, Verzenio, clears the FDA this month, as does GSK's Trelegy Ellipta, a triple drug, once daily inhaler for COPD. Solosec, a single-dose oral treatment for bacterial vaginosis expands new antibiotic options, and Bayer's Aliqopa for relapsed follicular lymphoma joins the booming oncology market. Plus, Amgen's Mvasi is the seventh approved U.S. biosimilar and the first for any cancer indication.
